Textbooks

Most graduate level classes will require some sort of textbook. These materials (rental, used, and new) are available for purchase at the UMSL TritonStore, located in 209 Millennium Student Center (2nd floor of the MSC).You can order your books in person or online at UMSL TritonStore. This site allows you to compare prices and purchase textbooks for pickup online. If the textbooks are not listed on the TritonStore's webpage, you should check your class syllabus on Canvas for textbook information. We suggest doing this early and before classes begin to avoid long lines and book runouts.

If you would like to add your textbooks to your UMSL bill, you must bring your UMSL TritonCard with you at the time of purchase.
